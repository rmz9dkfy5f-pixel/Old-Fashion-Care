## Variants worth testing
**Hero primary button**
- **B — "Talk to Regina — Free"** (soft/personal): names the human; lowers the "committing to a
  service" feeling. On a business whose whole pitch is *one trusted person*, naming her likely
  out-pulls a generic verb. Worth an A/B test vs. current.
- C — "Start With a Free Visit": "visit" reads less clinical than "consultation."

**Final contact panel heading**
- **F — "Let's talk about what your family needs."** (recommended): one-word change from current,
  more concrete, same voice, zero downside.
- G — "Not sure where to start? Let's talk.": empathy-led; meets the overwhelm the audience feels.

## Structural note
The homepage has CTAs only at the very top (hero) and very bottom (panel). Consider one **soft
mid-page CTA** after the "Meet Regina" section (e.g. "Have a question for Regina? Call
(919) 902-4150") to catch visitors who bounce before reaching the footer panel. Additive, not a
rewrite.
