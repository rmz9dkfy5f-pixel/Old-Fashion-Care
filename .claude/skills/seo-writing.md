# Skill: seo-writing

The writing system for all content-producing SEO commands. Ensures pages are rankable, citable,
and human — not AI slop.

## Extractability structure

- Open each section with a direct, self-contained answer to the section's question.
- Use question-shaped headings that match real search/AI queries.
- Keep key passages short and declarative so they make sense quoted out of context.
- Use tables/lists for comparisons and steps.

## Anti-AI-slop rules

- No empty throat-clearing ("In today's fast-paced world…", "It's important to note…").
- No vague filler adjectives; prefer specific nouns, numbers, and named examples.
- Vary sentence length; avoid the repetitive listicle cadence.
- Cut hedging that adds no information. Say the thing.
- No fabricated statistics, studies, or quotes — every stat needs a real source or gets removed.

## Proof-we-are-real signals

- First-hand experience, original data, named people, real photos, specific process detail.
- Cite sources for claims; link to primary sources where possible.
- Show, don't assert, expertise (specific numbers, edge cases, tradeoffs).

## Direct-answer formatting

- Answer the query in the first sentence under the heading, then elaborate.
- Put the most important information above the fold and above supporting detail.

## Specificity requirements

- Replace generic claims with concrete figures, timeframes, and examples.
- Prefer "reduced load time from 4.2s to 1.1s" over "improved performance."

## Pre-publish quality gate

Before a page is publish-ready, check:

- [ ] Direct answer present under each major heading
- [ ] No hallucinated stats/quotes; every claim sourced or removed
- [ ] No AI-slop phrasing or filler
- [ ] Specific proof and experience signals present
- [ ] Headings match real queries
- [ ] Scannable structure (short paras, lists, tables)
- [ ] Internal links to relevant money/related pages
- [ ] Title/meta reflect the actual promise of the page
