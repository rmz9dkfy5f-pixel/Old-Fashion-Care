# Skill: seo-aeo

How to get cited by AI answer surfaces — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and similar —
without lying about what's measurable. AEO = Answer Engine Optimization. Commands reference this
skill whenever the goal is extractability or AI citation.

## What actually improves extractability

- **Direct answers first.** Lead a section with a clear, self-contained answer to the question in
  the heading, then support it. AI answer engines quote passages that stand alone.
- **Question-shaped headings.** Use the real question a user asks as the H2/H3; put the answer in
  the first sentence beneath it.
- **Named, specific proof.** Statistics with sources, named examples, dates, and concrete numbers
  are more quotable than vague claims.
- **Structured data / schema.** FAQ, HowTo, Article, Product schema help machines parse the answer.
- **Quotable passages.** Short, declarative, complete sentences that make sense out of context.
- **Clear entity definitions.** Define the thing plainly ("X is a …") so the model can attribute.
- **Tables and lists** for comparisons and steps — easy to extract and reproduce.

## What is hype or unmeasurable

- Claims that a specific tweak "guarantees" AI citation — attribution is opaque and changes often.
- Precise "AI traffic share" numbers unless you have a real measurement method.
- Keyword-density tricks; answer engines reward clarity and proof, not stuffing.
- Any promise of ranking in a named AI product on a timeline.

## How to measure AI citations without lying

- Manually query the target questions in the AI surfaces and record whether/how the site is cited.
- Track branded and unbranded prompts over time; log citation presence, not vanity metrics.
- Where an API/analytics source exists (e.g. referral traffic from an AI product), use it and label
  it clearly. Otherwise state that measurement is manual and periodic.
- Never fabricate citation counts or "AI visibility scores."

## Why the fundamentals matter

Direct answers, question headings, statistics, named proof, schema, and quotable passages all make
a page easier for a machine to lift and attribute. Optimize for being the clearest, most
verifiable answer to a real question — that's the durable strategy across changing AI surfaces.
