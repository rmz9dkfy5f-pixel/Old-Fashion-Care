About Old Fashion Care

Old Fashion Care is a care coordination service. Regina vets, interviews, and personally selects certified self-employed care professionals — CNAs, PCAs, licensed cosmetologists, and certified pedicurists — and matches them to your family's needs. She manages the relationship and remains your point of contact throughout. OFC does not directly employ the caregivers it refers.
Yes. Old Fashion Care, LLC is licensed as a home care coordinator in the state of North Carolina in accordance with NC Division of Health Service Regulation requirements.
Regina is the founder and sole coordinator of Old Fashion Care. She holds a Bachelor's degree in long-term care, is a Certified Nursing Assistant, a Certified Medication Aid, a Certified Activity Coordinator, and served as a licensed facility administrator from 2016 to 2018. Every client relationship at OFC is managed personally by Regina.
Old Fashion Care serves Raleigh and the surrounding Wake County area. Call us to confirm availability in a specific neighborhood or ZIP code.
